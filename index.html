About Dietitian Maryam

Dietitian Maryam Shahrukh is a qualified and passionate clinical nutritionist dedicated to improving lives through healthy and sustainable dietary habits.

She believes that every individual is unique, and therefore, each diet plan should be personalized according to lifestyle, health conditions, and personal goals.

Her approach focuses on long-term health improvement rather than temporary or extreme dieting methods.
